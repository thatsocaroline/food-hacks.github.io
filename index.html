3. Make potato chips in your microwave.

  1. Slice potatoes into 1/8 inch thick
  2. Quickly rinse the potatoes in water to remove any starch.
  3. Dry the potatoes
  4. Arrange as many potato slices on the paper towels as will fit in one layer. Make sure they don't touch.
  5. Microwave at 100% power for 3 min.
  6. Flip them over and microwave at 50% power for 3 more minutes.
  7. Flip and continue cooking at 50% power in 1 minute intervals until the chips start to turn golden.

4. 2-Ingredient Ice Cream

  1. Combine half-and-half, sugar, and vanilla extract in a small resealable plastic bag.
  2. Fill a large resealable plastic bag with ice and salt.
  3. Place the small bag inside the larger bag and shake vigorously for about 5 minutes, until ice cream has hardened.
